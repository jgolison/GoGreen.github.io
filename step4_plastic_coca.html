Coca Cola’s revolutionary new plastic that is fully recyclable sounds incredible! Unfortunately it simply isn’t true. The PlantBottles contained a maximum of 15% recycled plastic, a small percentage and certainly not the 30% they boasted. Don’t let these corporate greenwashing publicity stunts fool you! Stop buying single-use plastic, and stop buying into the false advertising.
