You FAILED....

Starbucks’ ‘recyclable’ and revolutionary drink lid falls quite short of its expectations. For starters, this lid will almost never actually get recycled. The type of plastic used gets recycled about 5% of the time. Starbucks gives out over 8,000 single-use cups every minute. Only a miniscule percentage of these cups will end up in the recycling bin and only a miniscule percentage of those cups will even get recycled. Don’t let these corporation greenwashing publicity stunts fool you! Avoid buying beverages in single-use plastics, research companies that let you bring in reusable cups, or make coffe at home!
